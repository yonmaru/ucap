UNION CAPITAL GROUP

Union Capital Group is an alliance of international banking, investment advisory, structured finance and brokerage firms focused on custom institutional solutions for global investors and their trusted advisors.

We work together to service our clients internationally. Each firm is registered and regulated as required in its home country. Our aim is to provide innovative solutions in a world where corporate control across countries have made global banks inflexible and bureaucratic, leading to arbitrary policies detrimental to clients and their advisors.
